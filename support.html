eFast Paste 技术支持

最后更新日期：2026 年 7 月 9 日

eFast Paste 是一款本地优先的 macOS 剪贴板历史工具。如果你在安装、使用、订阅或恢复购买时遇到问题，可以参考下面的说明。

常见问题

1. 复制的内容没有出现在 eFast Paste 中怎么办？

  • 确认 eFast Paste 正在运行。
  • 打开设置，确认没有开启“暂停剪贴板记录”。
  • 如果复制的是图片或视频文件，确认设置中对应的记录开关已开启。

2. 为什么 App Store 版本不能自动粘贴到其他应用？

Mac App Store 版本遵循沙盒和审核要求，只负责把内容复制到系统剪贴板。复制后，请在目标应用中手动按 Command + V 粘贴。

3. 图片、视频、文件重启后还能复制吗？

可以。App Store 版本会在本机应用目录保存一份文件缓存，用于重启后继续显示和复制。再次复制到 Finder 或其他位置时，会尽量保留原文件名。

4. 订阅或永久买断后没有立即生效怎么办？

  1. 打开 eFast Paste 设置。
  2. 点击“刷新”检查 Pro 状态。
  3. 如果换电脑或重装过应用，点击“恢复购买”。
  4. 确认你登录的是购买时使用的 Apple ID。

5. 如何删除剪贴板历史？

你可以在应用内删除单条记录、清空今日前记录或清空全部记录。也可以在设置中配置自动清理。

联系支持

如果以上内容不能解决问题，请通过 App Store 产品页提供的开发者联系方式联系开发者。反馈问题时建议包含：

  • macOS 版本。
  • eFast Paste 版本号。
  • 问题发生前的操作步骤。
  • 是否来自 TestFlight / App Store 版本。
  • 相关截图或错误提示。

eFast Paste Support

Last updated: July 9, 2026

eFast Paste is a local-first macOS clipboard history utility. If you need help with installation, usage, subscriptions, or purchase restoration, please refer to the information below.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Clipboard items do not appear in eFast Paste

  • Make sure eFast Paste is running.
  • Open Settings and make sure clipboard recording is not paused.
  • If you copied images or video files, make sure the corresponding recording options are enabled.

2. Why can't the App Store version paste automatically into other apps?

The Mac App Store version follows sandbox and review requirements. It copies content to the system clipboard, and users paste manually with Command + V in the target app.

3. Can image, video, and file records still be copied after restart?

Yes. The App Store version stores local file copies in the app data directory so records can still be displayed and copied after restart. When copied back to Finder or another location, eFast Paste preserves the original filename when possible.

4. Subscription or lifetime purchase did not unlock immediately

  1. Open eFast Paste Settings.
  2. Click Refresh to update Pro status.
  3. If you reinstalled the app or changed Mac, click Restore Purchases.
  4. Make sure you are signed in with the Apple ID used for purchase.

5. How do I delete clipboard history?

You can delete individual records, clear records before today, or clear all records in the app. You can also configure automatic cleanup in Settings.

Contact Support

If the information above does not resolve your issue, please contact the developer through the contact information provided on the App Store product page. When reporting an issue, include your macOS version, eFast Paste version, steps to reproduce, whether you are using TestFlight or the App Store version, and any relevant screenshots or error messages.
